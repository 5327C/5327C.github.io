Our Team


Team 5327C is from Dublin High School of Dublin, California. We work together with other members of our school's Gael Force Robotics club.We strive to design, build, program, and test an effective and efficient robot. Over the course of the 2014-2015 VEX season, we hope to not only develop an exceptional robot, but also strengthen our engineering skills, and forge lasting ties with other members of the robotics community.
