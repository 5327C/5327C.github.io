Last year, I was on D team and temporarily on A team, while they were at worlds. I am hoping that this year will be as good as last yar. I am originally from Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. I moved here because my dad got a job in the United States. I am interested in engineering and I am currently in the engineering academy at our school.This is what brought me into robotics, since some of my friends had joined and told me about how fun it is. Back in Canada, I was on an FLL team for two years. On that team I was able to go to the world competition. This helps for getting used to the work environment, and exposes you to real world experiences. Throughout this VEX season, I hope we will have a successful and prosperous year in our robotics performance and robotics community.
